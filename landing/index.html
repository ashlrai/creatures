STDP Plasticity

Not just evolution —
real learning

Organisms don't just evolve across generations. They learn within their lifetime through spike-timing-dependent plasticity — the same mechanism your brain uses right now.

PRE
synapse strengthens
POST

When the presynaptic neuron fires before the postsynaptic neuron, the connection gets stronger. Hebbian learning at the spike level.

+13.5%
motor response improvement over 10 trials

Synapses that fire together, wire together

STDP is the biological learning rule that governs real neural plasticity. When a presynaptic spike arrives just before the postsynaptic neuron fires, that synapse strengthens. The reverse weakens it. This is how brains learn.

  • +Within-lifetime learning — organisms improve their responses without any evolution
  • +Motor response strengthening — a worm's touch withdrawal improves 13.5% over 10 trials
  • +Biologically accurate — the same exponential STDP window used in computational neuroscience
  • +Evolution + Learning — organisms evolve their architecture AND learn within each lifetime

Built on Real Science

Standing on the shoulders
of neuroscience

Every neuron, every synapse, every connection weight comes from peer-reviewed connectomics data and biophysical models.

Connectome Data

OpenWorm / WormAtlas

Complete C. elegans connectome: 302 neurons (299 non-pharyngeal), 6,393 chemical synapses, 890 gap junctions. White et al. (1986), updated by Varshney et al. (2011).

FlyWire Connectome

Drosophila Brain (FlyWire v783)

500-neuron subset from the complete Drosophila melanogaster brain connectome. Whole-brain electron microscopy reconstruction by Dorkenwald et al. (2023), Nature.

Neural Simulation

Brian2 + Cython Backend

LIF neuron models with biologically realistic membrane dynamics and synaptic transmission. Compiled Cython backend for real-time performance. Stimberg et al. (2019), eLife.

Physics Engine

MuJoCo + NeuroMechFly

Multi-joint dynamics with contact for C. elegans locomotion. NeuroMechFly biomechanical model for Drosophila. Todorov et al. (2012); Lobato-Rios et al. (2022).

Regulatory

FDA Modernization Act 2.0

Signed December 2022 — removed the mandate for animal testing in drug development. Opens the door for in-silico validation using platforms like Neurevo.

Pharmacology

Hill Equation Kinetics

Dose-response modeling with Hill coefficients for 8 characterized compounds. Receptor-level interactions with real ion channel dynamics and neurotransmitter pathways.
